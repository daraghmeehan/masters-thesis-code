The Inuit languages are spoken in the Arctic area. The term Eskimo was used in the past, replaced today by Inuit.

There are several groups of dialects:
- Alaska: inupiaq,
- Canada: inuktitut (Nunavut, North Québec), inuvialuktun (Northwest Territories), inuinnaqtun (West Nunavut)
- Greenland: kalaallisut.
